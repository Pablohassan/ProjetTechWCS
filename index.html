About The Kindom

Colchis is bordered on the south-west by Pontus, on the west by the Black Sea, as far as the river Corax. To its north is the Greater Caucasus, beyond which is Sarmatia. On its east it border the Kingdom of Iberia and Montes Moschici. The south of Colchis bordered Armenia.
